Calibrate Device Pointing

Your device contains sensors that allow it to determine where it is pointing. Those sensors are easily used to calculate how far "up" you are pointing (i.e. your Elevation), however due to browser compatibiliy challenges they are more difficult to use to determine how far North/East/South/West you are pointing (i.e. your Azimuth)

The workaround is that we need to "calibrate" the device pointing with the known (approximate is OK) direction of North using the steps below (sorry, you will need to calibrate any time you lock your device):

  1. Press the "Start" button below
  2. Place your device face-up on a table
  3. Rotate your device so the top is pointing North
  4. Press the "Calibrate" button
